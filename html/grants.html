Grants

Applications are open for grants of $50,000 in the following areas :

1_Supporting the livelihoods of vulnerable groups in society through the implementation of projects that serve the agriculture or livestock sector or support the economy.

2_Protecting women and reducing violence against women through the implementation of projects aimed at empowering them economically.

Applications are received during the period from October 15, 2021 to November 20, 2021


Application conditions:

  • Charities and local organizations working in the humanitarian field.
  • must have a license to practice work from the local authorities and attach it to the application for grant.
  • The applicant must have at least one year of experience in the above-mentioned fields.
  • The submitted application includes (an overview of the project to be implemented - description of the context - main activities - outputs and objectives must be clearly defined - monitoring and evaluation plan - sustainability plan) and be in pdf format and attached to the budget in an excel table.
  • All policies of the association or organization (financial, logistical, human resources, follow-up and evaluation, internal system and organizational structure) must be attached. 6- All documents must be sent in English or Arabic to the following email: Grants@IRF.org

Three months after the closing date Accepted applicants will be contacted.

We will work to be partnerships with those accepted and prepare good future plans.
